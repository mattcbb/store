Fondant, Food Coloring and More

We carry Wilton Decorator Preferred fondant in a variety of colors, as well as fondant rollers and other tools of the trade.

We also have AmeriColor food coloring, which is the best on the market. It's a gel, which makes it easier to use than regular food coloring. We have both regular and oil-based colors.

In addition, we have dozens of decorating tips, decorating bags, and anything else you could need to make the perfect cake. Call or stop by for more details.
